Defense & Government

Policy Analysis Pipeline

Developed quantitative analysis tools for major defense agencies and federal organizations.

Analytics Data Engineering Research

Key Outcomes

  • — Automated analysis of large policy datasets
  • — Reduced analysis time from weeks to hours
  • — Supported major defense procurement decisions

The Challenge

Defense agencies needed to analyze vast amounts of procurement data, contracts, and policy documents to make informed decisions. Traditional analysis methods couldn’t keep up with the volume.

The Approach

At RAND and Govini, I developed quantitative analysis pipelines that could process and synthesize large policy datasets. The systems combined statistical analysis, natural language processing, and visualization to surface insights.

Technical Highlights

  • Built ETL pipelines for diverse government data sources
  • Developed statistical models for trend analysis and forecasting
  • Created interactive dashboards for policy analysts
  • Implemented reproducible research workflows

The Outcome

The tools enabled analysts to process data that would have taken weeks in just hours, supporting critical procurement and policy decisions.
