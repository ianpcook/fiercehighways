## The Challenge

Insurance underwriting requires extracting accurate, structured data from thousands of complex, unstructured documents daily — applications, medical records, financial statements. Manual processing was the bottleneck, and the stakes for errors are high: regulatory exposure, missed risk signals, bad pricing.

## The Approach

As Head of AI at Sixfold, I engineered an enterprise-scale LLM platform purpose-built for insurance document processing, with compliance and accuracy as first-order concerns.

## Key Contributions

- **Engineered enterprise-scale LLM platform** processing 10,000+ insurance documents daily, slashing underwriter review time by 97%
- **Formulated comprehensive PII/PHI protection protocols** that exceeded HIPAA and SOC 2 Type 2 compliance requirements
- **Developed proprietary LLM evaluation framework** that slashed hallucination rates, ensuring extraction reliability at scale
- **Created customized document analysis workbench**, increasing extraction accuracy to above 90%

## The Impact

The platform transformed underwriting from a document-heavy manual process into an AI-assisted workflow where underwriters focus on judgment calls rather than data entry — with the compliance guardrails to operate in a regulated industry.
