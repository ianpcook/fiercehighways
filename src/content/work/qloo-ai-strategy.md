## The Context

Qloo is a cultural intelligence platform that maps human taste across 3.7B+ lifestyle entities — music, dining, fashion, travel, and more — using privacy-preserving AI. I joined as SVP of AI to accelerate the platform's evolution and open new market channels.

## What I Do

I lead AI strategy and engineering execution, focusing on making Qloo's intelligence accessible through modern AI interfaces and expanding its commercial reach.

## Key Contributions

- **Pioneered Model Context Protocol (MCP) server** enabling integration with major LLM platforms, expanding Qloo's market reach into the agentic AI ecosystem
- **Architected provider-agnostic multi-LLM infrastructure**, reducing single-vendor dependency and increasing system resilience
- **Engineered new optimization models** with LLM-based synthesis, improving recommendation quality
- **Transformed development practices** by implementing rigorous code standards and orchestrating synchronized sprint cadences across engineering teams, accelerating release cycles
- **Secured significant contract expansion** with American Express through innovative data model demonstrations and strategic executive presentations

## The Impact

These initiatives are positioning Qloo at the intersection of cultural intelligence and the emerging agentic AI landscape — making taste data accessible not just through APIs, but through the AI platforms developers and enterprises are already building on.
